About Jongoler Golpo

World Best Resort In Wild Nature

Welcome to “Jongoler Golpo” Step into a world where nature meets adventure and family fun. Relax in peaceful hammocks, enjoy scenic boat rides, or feel the thrill of jongoler rope walking and tracking. Every corner of our park tells a story, making unforgettable memories for visitors of all ages. Discover, play, and celebrate – experience the Jongoler like never before!
